About Us

viewnex is a free, independent YouTube analytics tool I built to make it easier to check any channel’s statistics, video performance, and estimated revenue.


It was created as a simple side project because I wanted a fast and clean way to analyze YouTube channels without complicated dashboards or paid subscriptions. The tool pulls public data directly from YouTube and shows real numbers like views, estimated earnings, and basic growth insights.

What you should know:

  • It is completely free with no login or limits
  • Revenue estimates are approximate (actual earnings can be higher or lower)
  • We do not store any of your searches or personal data
  • This is a small independent project, not a big company

My goal is simple: to provide useful and transparent YouTube analytics that anyone can use — whether you're a creator trying to understand your channel or just curious about someone else’s.


Thank you for using viewnex.
